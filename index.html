    Roger Samuda Asquith (born August 3. 2002) is a student at Penn State University (Berks). He began his Academic Career at Penn State Berks

    Career

    2018-current : Current Career

    Asquith' career began in 2018 when he started working at Phoebe Berks as a Dietitan Assistant for three years. In 2022, Asquith started working as a Student IT Consultant at Penn State Berks .[14]
